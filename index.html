About GyaanBotics

GyaanBotics Private Limited is an education-focused technology startup dedicated to transforming how students learn Robotics, Artificial Intelligence, IoT and 3D Design.

We work closely with schools and institutions to design NEP 2020-aligned, hands-on, skill-based learning programs that build logical thinking, creativity and real-world problem-solving skills.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between traditional education and future industry needs by providing complete end-to-end STEM solutions — from lab infrastructure and curriculum to trained faculty and ongoing support.
