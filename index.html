About GyaanBotics

Transforming India’s educational landscape through innovative Robotics, Artificial Intelligence, IoT and emerging technology solutions.

Creating Centers of Excellence Across India

GyaanBotics Private Limited is an education-focused technology startup at the forefront of STEM innovation. We specialize in Robotics, AI, IoT and 3D Design education, partnering with K-12 schools, universities and institutions.

Our approach goes beyond conventional training. We design comprehensive learning ecosystems that combine modern infrastructure, progressive curriculum and expert faculty to prepare students for real-world technology challenges.

By aligning closely with NEP 2020, we ensure institutions deliver future-ready education while maintaining academic excellence and industry relevance.

Mission

To democratize access to advanced technology education and create centers of excellence in Robotics and AI across India’s educational institutions.

Vision

To be India’s leading EdTech partner, empowering every student with future-ready skills in AI, Robotics and emerging technologies.

Impact

Transforming education across institutions by integrating cutting-edge technology, trained faculty and industry-aligned learning models.
